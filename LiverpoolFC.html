Liverpool FC

"Liverpool was made for me and I was made for Liverpool" - Bill Shankly

Liverpool FC is an English football clup which was first formed in 1892. In modern day, it is now one of the most successful clubs in Europes. It has won the Champions League 5 times throughout its history in 1977, 1978, 1981, 1984 and lastly and most know famous 2005 Istanbul victory.

Liverpool Manager: Jurgen Klopp

December, 2015

'Introducing a New Manager'

It has been almost 2 years since Brendan Rodgers was relieved of his dutie at Liverpool, but his legacy should not be the subject.A rather insipid performance at Goodison Park had seen Liverpool scrape a 1-1 draw with Everton, and the writing was on the wall for Rodgers.

October 4, 2015 repersented a day that had been on the cards for a while, after drawing rivals Everton, Brendan Rodgers was shortly sacked. To Liverpools relieve, Jurgen Klopp was shortly appointed the new tasking job.

Jurgen Klopp was a skillful and thoughtful manager. He had transformed liverpool in the last 12 months and in comparison to Brendan Rodgers who is thought to be a laughing stock in the eyes of many supporters.

Though it may be against the popular wave of opinion, Rodgers deserves to be respected for some of the feats he achieved on Merseyside. Liverpool have been done well this season beating rivals Arsenal and Manchester City. It is thought that Jurgen Klopp will be aiming to get the reds top 4 this season
